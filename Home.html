About this App


  • Location-Based Functionality: The app determines the user's current position as the starting point, making navigation personalized and relevant.

  • Interactive Interface: Users can simply click their desired destination within DeKUT, making it user-friendly and straightforward to use.

  • Route Generation: Automatically creates the optimal path from the user's location to their selected destination on campus.

  • Turn-by-Turn Directions: Provides clear navigation instructions to help users follow the generated route to reach their destination.
